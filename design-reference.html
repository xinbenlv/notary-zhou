New to notarization?

第一次办理公证？

Think of a notary as an impartial witness. We verify your identity, ensure you are signing willingly, and observe the signing of your document. Do not sign your document before we meet.

What to bring:

  • The complete document (unsigned)
  • Valid Government-issued ID (Driver's License or Passport)
  • Payment (Cash, Venmo, Zelle, or Credit Card)
